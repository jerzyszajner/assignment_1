Biocca

New technologies and features designed for the most discerning coffee connoisseurs

Main Features

  • Dual boiler
  • Touch screen display
  • Push buttons
  • Textless interface
  • Design philosophy

Rocket’s relentless pursuit of improvement involves rigorous analysis of new technologies, challenging historical traditions, and embracing innovative experimental methods. The outcome: Bicocca, an espresso machine and experience designed for the most discerning coffee connoisseurs.

The Bicocca’s bold architecture is a visual cue to the machines internal engineering that is designed to exceed the needs of even the most demanding home barista.

The design pays homage to it’s technical capabilities with an intuitive touch-sensitive interface placed on the upper face of the group head. This allows even more refined control of the coffee delivery, the cornerstone of professional level performance.

Dual stainless steel boilers. Rotary pump with water reservoir or direct water connection. Stainless steel or matte black finishing options.
